Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can you do off-page SEO as well?

Yes I can perform Off-page SEO as well and also Technical On page SEO, off page supplements the page optimization and make it more effective for google top ranking.

2. Which tools do you use?

Ahref, Moz, SEMRush, screamingfrog. In case you would like me to use any other tool, you can let me know. These tools help for proper page optimization, technical onpage SEO and push to google top ranking

3. What are the 4 types of SEO?

Onpage SEO (on-site SEO) Off-page SEO (off-site SEO) Technical SEO. Local SEO. all this includes page optimization which helps on ranking

4. Why is technical SEO so important?

Technical SEO and page optimization is important because it essentially ensures that your website is easy to navigate and is free of any technical issues that prevent it from being understood and ranked by search engines. It helps you to come on google top ranking.

5. What is onpage Optimization?

Onpage SEO optimization refers to the practice of optimizing webpages to improve a website's search engine rankings and earn organic traffic. In addition to publishing relevant, high-quality content, onpage SEO includes optimizing your headlines, HTML tags (title, meta, and header)

6. Which is example of onpage SEO?

Example of some onpage SEO action items include: Optimizing your title tags and meta descriptions. Writing in-depth, quality content. Cleaning up your site's code. on page deals specially with page optimization and boost ranking. Technical SEO, page Optimization is under Onpage SEO

7. What is YouTube SEO video optimization?

As you probably know, keyword optimization is a KEY part of YouTube SEO. So if a video has lots and lots of views, chances are that the video is optimized around a popular keyword.

8. How long does SEO take to work? When will I get traffic and clicks?

YouTube SEO involves optimizing your channel, metadata, title, description, tags, and the videos themselves. SEO is a long-term strategy for consistent channel growth but does take some time and patience. SEO alone usually takes about 3-4 weeks for YouTube to re-index the changes.
